Hello World!

2019/03/15

Hi. Welcome to my first blog post. This is a place for me to journal about anything and everything.

As a student, it can be easy to become absorbed in whatever you're studying. They say engineers can't spell, or articulate themselves in a non-technical way for that matter. As much as I hate to admit, writing this was exasperatingly difficult. All of a sudden putting two words together requires . . . actual effort? Believe it or not, I actually enjoyed English class in high school; I even find myself missing it sometimes. But wait. An engineer who likes words? Likes the subjectivty of interpreting literature? Finds joy in using . . . figurative language?? How amusing. Moreover, how contrary to the rules of engineering design :p

I know that dedicating myself to the study of engineering may shutter certain aspects of my identity. This space gives me a medium which I can use to reconcile that. And hey, maybe I'll gain an internet presence in the process. As I've settled down into my life in university and the busy city of Toronto, I decided it would be comforting to have a place like this to write freely about whatever I'd like.

I don't know how or why you've gotten this far. But if you have, here are some of the subjects you can look forward to reading about: music(all sorts), programming(all sorts), and all sorts of other things(idk yet). Keep in touch.
